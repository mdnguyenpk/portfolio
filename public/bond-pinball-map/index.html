The two headline multiballs

Bird 1 Multiball (3-ball)

Qualify

Knock down the 3 drop targets in front of the rocket to light the center loop lock, then shoot the center loop 3 times to lock all 3 balls and start it.

Rules

Score lit jackpots at the rocket. Make 3 jackpot shots from the upper flipper before time runs out to extend the multiball, and keep scoring jackpots to light the super jackpot at the rocket. Completing the 7 SPECTRE standups before starting raises every jackpot value.

Jetpack Multiball (2-ball)

Qualify

Shoot the left eject (the hidden scoop under the upper flipper) to light the left ramp, then shoot the left ramp to start it.

Rules

Shoot the center loop right away to lock in a 3M starting jackpot, then hit lit jackpots. After 5, the left ramp lights a double jackpot, then a super jackpot plus add-a-ball at a shot moving right to left. On Premium/LE, that left ramp shot sends the jetpack over to the Dragon Tank automatically — shoot the tank target once it arrives to drop the locked ball for the super jackpot.

Skill shots — 8 named shots available every ball; hold left flipper + full plunge to access the timed shots

Undercover

  • How — Hold left flipper, full plunge, shoot the right ramp within 5 switch hits.
  • Awards — 2.5M + 500k per attempt, plus one Smart Missile (once per game).

Double "O"

  • How — Hold left flipper, full plunge, shoot the side ramp within 5 switch hits.
  • Awards — 2.5M + 500k per attempt, plus one Smart Missile (once per game).

Eyes Only

  • How — Hold left flipper, full plunge, shoot the DB5 eject within 5 switch hits.
  • Awards — 2.5M + 500k per attempt, plus one Smart Missile (once per game).

Top Secret

  • How — Hold left flipper, full plunge, shoot the bumper lane from the upper flipper.
  • Awards — 2.5M + 500k per attempt, plus one Smart Missile (once per game).

Licensed To Kill

  • How — Shoot the left eject directly off the initial plunge.
  • Awards — 2.5M + 500k per attempt, plus one Smart Missile (once per game).

Commander Bond

  • How — Medium-strength plunge, then shoot the left ramp.
  • Awards — 1.25M + 250k per attempt. No Smart Missile.

Classified

  • How — Direct plunge to the flashing MI6 lane without using flippers to adjust trajectory.
  • Awards — 500k + 200k per attempt, plus +1x bonus multiplier.

MI6

  • How — Plunge toward the flashing MI6 lane; flippers may be used to redirect.
  • Awards — 300k + 100k per attempt, plus +1x bonus multiplier.

Super Skill Shot

Full plunge, then chain right orbit → upper loop → side ramp in sequence within 5 switch hits. Awards 2 Smart Missiles at once.

Martini Shot

Shoot the side ramp and let the ball roll back down the right ramp without completing the shot. Awards 1 Smart Missile plus 1M cumulative — the value grows by 1M for each Martini Shot collected across the game.

Action button — press to spend one of up to 4 stacked functions, each lit a different color

Smart Missile (blue)

  • Activate — Press (don't hold) when the button flashes blue.
  • Effect — Collects any lit shot automatically, prioritizing the hardest available shot.
  • Limits — Max 4 stacked. Disabled while a super jackpot is lit.
  • Sources — 1 free at game start; earned from super skill shots, mode completions, Martini Shot, and Swimming With Sharks weapon (grants 2 per ball).
  • Tip — If 007 Scoring is active, press either flipper to switch the button to missile mode without cashing out the multiplier.

007 Scoring (pink)

  • Activate — Press when the button is pink after collecting one or more Bond Women.
  • Effect — Activates all collected women at once as a playfield multiplier (+1x per woman, up to +7x).
  • Timer — 10s base +10s per woman (20s at 2x up to 70s at 7x). Reaching 7x also grants a 30s ball save.
  • Tip — Time it to land during a jackpot barrage — entering Bird 1 Multiball at 7x is the highest-value play on the machine.

Add a Ball (yellow)

  • Activate — Hold the action button during multiball when lit yellow.
  • Effect — Adds an extra ball into play during any multiball.
  • Source — Awarded by completing the Outer Space Hijack SPECTRE weapon (You Only Live Twice).
  • Limits — Mutually exclusive with Swipe a Ball on Premium/LE — you can only use one per multiball.

Swipe a Ball (green — Premium/LE only)

  • Activate — Press when lit green during Bird 1 Multiball.
  • Effect — Retrieves a physically locked ball from the habitrail and puts it back into play.
  • Limits — Once per multiball only. Each subsequent Bird 1 start requires more drop target completions before this becomes available again.
  • Note — Mutually exclusive with Add a Ball on the same multiball.

"Bond… James Bond" multiball — the low-level wizard mode

Bond… James Bond multiball

Qualify

Play at least one villain mode, one henchmen mode, and start both Bird 1 and Jetpack Multiball. Once all four are done, the right ramp and left eject light to start it.

Format

2-ball multiball lasting just over a minute. Make every shot on the playfield twice to progress — upper loop shots score double and count toward two shots at once.

Payoff

Once every shot has been made twice, the side ramp lights for the super jackpot — up to 100M unmultiplied, and gone the instant time runs out.

Tip

Carry a 7x 007 Scoring multiplier into this mode and scores over a billion are possible — a great early goal for beginners or a safe way to lock in a strong tournament score.

Scoring priority guide

1

Start a villain and a henchmen mode early

Right ramp and side ramp — this plus both multiballs qualifies "Bond… James Bond" multiball, an easy 100M-cap payday.

2

Stack a mode into Bird 1 or Jetpack Multiball

Jackpots run 3M+ each and Bird 1's super jackpot caps at 300M — always more valuable inside multiball than solo.

3

Bank Bond Women, then cash 007 Scoring mid-multiball

Up to 7x playfield for up to 70 seconds — time it to land on a jackpot barrage, not an empty shot.

4

Hit SPECTRE targets before starting Bird 1

Each target adds 25k to every jackpot for that multiball — worth clearing first if you can.

5

Bank smart missiles for hard shots

Super skill shots and mode completions build a stock of up to 4 — spend on the shot you'd otherwise miss.

6

Chase all four modes per film for bonus

Completing villain, henchmen, SPECTRE weapon, and Q Branch for one film adds 25M to end-of-ball bonus.

7

Push toward a villain mini-wizard, then the final wizard mode

Starting all six of any mode type unlocks a mini-wizard; clearing all four unlocks On Her Majesty's Secret Service.
